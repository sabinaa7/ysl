About Saint Laurent, Yves

Biography about Yves Henri Donat Mathieu-Saint-Laurent.

From 1955 to 1957 he worked at the fashion house of Christian Dior and was one of his assistants.
Around the end of the 1980s, he began to get increasingly ill and was treated for alcohol and drug addiction.
In 1964 he released his first perfume called Y.
In 1981, Yves Saint Laurent received the International Award of the Council of Fashion Designers of America.


Yves Saint Laurent (YSL) is a famous French fashion house founded in 1961 by designer Yves Saint Laurent and his partner Pierre Bergé. Known for its innovative and revolutionary approach, YSL has had a significant impact on the fashion world. Yves Saint Laurent aimed to challenge traditional fashion norms and introduce new, modern styles. He became famous for bringing elements of men’s fashion into women’s wear, like the iconic "le smoking" tuxedo suit for women, which symbolized empowerment and freedom for women.
